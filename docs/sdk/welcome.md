Memphis.dev is more than a broker. It's a new streaming stack. It significantly accelerates the development of real-time applications that require a streaming platform with high throughput, <span>low latency</span>, easy troubleshooting, <span>fast time-to-value </span>, minimal platform operations, and all the observability you can think of.
